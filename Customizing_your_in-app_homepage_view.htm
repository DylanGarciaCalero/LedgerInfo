Customizing your in-app homepage view

After signing up or logging in to Ledger, you will be asked to set your income and fixed expenses to calculate your available budget to help you start saving up money. Once the available budget is set, you can begin to allocate the funds to expense categories. You can also create an additional category that suits your lifestyle. The data you enter for your income, fixed expenses, savings, and categories will reflect on the homepage. Ledger aims to provide you with a customizable homepage for easy access to your spending.
