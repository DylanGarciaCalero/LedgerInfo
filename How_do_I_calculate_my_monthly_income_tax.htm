How do I calculate my monthly income tax?

If your employer does not collect income tax from your paycheque, you may be responsible for claiming and paying it independently.

Within Canada, each province has their own set of rules and laws that dictate how much income tax is collected, in conjunction with federal tax rates.

In order to get the best estimate for your taxes, it is recommended that you visit an online federal tax calculator, such as Canada Revenue Agency’s online tax calculator tool, seen here: https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/e-services/e-services-businesses/payroll-deductions-online-calculator.html.
