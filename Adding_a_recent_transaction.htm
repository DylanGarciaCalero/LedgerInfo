Adding a recent transaction

The Expenses view shows your spending at a daily transactional level . You can you can scroll to check your transaction details by day, week, or month. Recent spending requires users to input all of their spending data themselves. Therefore, you can search for an entry using a category or a keyword.

 

To add an expense, follow these steps:

 

1. Go to the homepage.

2. Click Expenses. 

3. Enter the name and amount of your recent spending from the Add expense section box.

4. Click Apply changes.

Note: a confirmation message will appear below the Add Expense button.

 

Ledger App Expenses View

After successfully adding a new expense, it will show on the Recent Spendings table in your homepage.
