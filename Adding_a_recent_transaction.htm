Adding a recent transaction

The Expenses view shows your spending at a transactional level . You can you can scroll to check your transaction details by day, week, or month. Recent spending requires users to input all of their spending data themselves. Therefore, you can search for an entry using a category or a keyword.

 

To add an expense, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the homepage.
  2. Enter the name and amount of your recent spending from the Add expense section.
  3. Select which category you want to put your new entry.
  4. Select Add Expense.

Note: a confirmation message will appear below the Add Expense button.

 

 

After successfully adding a new expense, it will show on the Recent spendings table in your homepage.
