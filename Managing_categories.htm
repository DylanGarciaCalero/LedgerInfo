Managing categories

To organize your expenses, Ledger organizes them into different categories, such as groceries and transportation. Categories are the terminology we use for expenses that consistently exist over time and don't change. 

 

There is no limit on how many categories you can add to group your expenses properly. By sorting your recent expenses accordingly, it is easier to calculate your total spending and view it by category. 

 

To add a new expense category, follow these steps:

 

1. Select Add new category from the category section on the homepage. 

Note: You will be directed to the New category page.

2. Enter the category name.

3. Choose the amount to allocate for the new category.

4. Select Add category.

 

You successfully added a new category and can now start organizing your entries.
