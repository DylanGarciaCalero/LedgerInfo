Viewing transactions by a date range

Ledger offers multiple options for viewing your transactions. You can choose between Today, This Week, This Month, or All-Time from the Expenses view. By filtering your transaction by date range, it will be easier to find the information you need.

 

To filter transactions by date range, follow these steps:

 

1. In your homepage view, navigate to Expenses.

2. Under Filter, choose between Today, This week, This month, or All time from the Time period section to filter your transactions.

Note: You can filter transactions by categories. For more information about categories, visit Managing categories.

 

You will now be able to view your expenses and transactions by the date you specified.
