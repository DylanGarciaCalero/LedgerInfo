Deleting you account

Deleting your account will result in the removal of all your personal information and transactions that you created in Ledger. If you create a new account using the same email, you will re-enter your spending details and develop new categories to organize your transactions.

To delete your account, follow these steps:

  1. Go to your homepage.
  2. Select Profile icon from the main menu.
  3. Select Edit.
  4. Choose Delete.
  5. Select Ok to confirm the action.

You successfully deleted your account. Ledger will remove your personal information and transaction details. You will not be able to log in again.
