What Ledger can do 

Ledger is a web-based application for tracking daily spending habits. When used as designed, Ledger shows users their spending habits in a visual way.

 

You use Ledger by manually inputting your spending habits and finances on a regular basis. Ledger takes this data and organizes it for you visually to see and understand. This helps you be more accountable and aware of your daily finances, helping you make responsible decisions with your money.
