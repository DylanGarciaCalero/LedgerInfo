Setting up a savings goal

A savings goal can be created within Ledger to help keep you on track and motivated. It is a visual reminder within your homepage view that shows the progress of how much you have saved and contributed into your goals.

To set up a savings goal, follow these steps:

  1. In the homepage, click Add Goal.
  2. In the date form, input the date you want to achieve your goal by.
  3. Input a description for your goal.
  4. Click Save.

You have created a savings goal that will show up each time you are in the homepage view.
