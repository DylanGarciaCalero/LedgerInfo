Resetting your password

If you have forgotten or lost your password, you can reset it. To change your password, follow these steps:

  1. On the Ledger website site, click Forgot your Password?
  2. Type your email address.
  3. Click Reset my password.

Note: you will be sent a link to your email address that will allow you to input a new password.

If you were unable to successfully change your email, make sure your web browser’s cache is cleared. Refer to “Clearing your cache and cookies” for information on clearing your cache.
