Clearing your cache and cookies

This section will help you use Ledger more effectively online. Your internet browser stores data in a cache, which are usually records of recently visited websites as temporary files. When your browserâ€™s cache is full or corrupted, it can cause problems with resetting your email or password.

Select your browser to learn how to clear your cache:

Chrome

  1. Open Chrome.
  2. Click More.
  3. Select More tools > Clear browsing data.
  4. Pick the timeframe, like All time, or Today.
  5. Click the type of data you want removed.
  6. Select Clear data.

Internet Explorer

  1. In Internet Explorer, click Tools.
  2. Select Safety.
  3. Choose Delete browsing history.
  4. Click More.
  5. Select More tools > Clear browsing data.
  6. Pick the timeframe, like All time, or Today.
  7. Click the type of data you want removed.
  8. Select Clear data.

Mozilla Firefox

  1. Within Firefox, select the Menu button.
  2. Click Settings.
  3. Select Privacy & Security
  4. Find Cookies and Site Data section and click Clear Data.
  5. De-select Cookies and Site Data.
  6. Select Cached Web Content.
  7. Click Clear.

Safari

  1. Open the Safari app, select History > Clear History.
  2. Click the pop-up menu.
  3. Select each item and website you want removed.
