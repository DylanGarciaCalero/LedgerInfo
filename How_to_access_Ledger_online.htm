How to access Ledger online

Ledger is available online only, and does not exist as an app-store app. This means you cannot download Ledger to any mobile phone.

Instead, use an internet browser on your phone, laptop, or desktop to access Ledger. Follow these steps:

  1. Open your preferred browser on your device.
  2. In the address bar, insert the URL and navigate to the Ledger Homepage.

You will be able to Sign up or Login to your account.
