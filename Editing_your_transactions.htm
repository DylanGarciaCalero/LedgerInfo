Editing your transactions

To manage your spending and transaction, Ledger allows you to edit and delete your previous entries to calculate your monthly expenditures accurately. You can change the name and amount of your expenses entry and also assign them into a different category. By adjusting your spendings, you get to stick to your budget to reach your monetary goals.

To edit an expense entry, follow these steps:

 

1. In your homepage, go to Expenses.

2. Click the transaction you want to edit from the spending table under the Time period section.

3. Edit the amount, category, or name.

4. Click Save.

 

The edited entry will immediately reflect on the spendings table, and your available budget and savings will recalculate to reflect the changes you made.
