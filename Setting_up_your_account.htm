Setting up your account

After navigating to Ledger’s website address, you can login if you have an account. If you do not have an account, follow these steps:

  1. On Ledger’s main page, put your name and email address.

    Note: After you complete this entire process, you will receive an email with a link to confirm your account.

  2. Select a password and input it into the form.

    Note: Select a complex password with a randomized collection of numbers, letters, and symbols. Always keep your password private.

  3. Set your currency type to Canadian Dollars.
  4. Under Income, input your monthly income.

    Note: this monthly amount will be added automatically to your available budget.

  5. Under Expenses, input your fixed expenses.

    Note: Fixed expenses are on-going and predictable expenses you pay every month, like rent, electricity, or a car payment.

  6. In Savings, toggle the slider to the area on the line that indicates how much you want to save monthly.
  7. Navigate to the next page by selecting Next Step.

    Note: This page will show your Overview of the financial information you set up.

  8. Under Groceries and Transportation, toggle the slider to the amount you want to be allocated monthly for each of these budget categories.
  9. When finished, select Complete.

Now you have successfully set up your account.
