Setting up your account

After navigating to Ledger’s website address, you can login if you have an account.

 

If you do not have an account, follow these steps:

 

1. On Ledger's log in page, click Sign Up.

Note: After you complete this entire process, you will receive an email with a link to confirm your account.

2. Add your name, email,  and choose a password.

Note: Select a complex password with a randomized collection of numbers, letters, and symbols. Refer to Protecting your account to learn about password protection.

3. Click Sign up. 

Note: A new screen will appear wher eyou can enter your financial information.

4. Under Income, set your monthly income amount and select a monthly date. 

Note: This monthly amount will be added automatically to your available budget.

5. Under Expenses, input your fixed expenses. 

Note: Fixed expenses are on-going and predictable expense you pay every month, like rent, electricity, or a car payment.

6. In Savings, toggle the slide to the area on the line that indicates how much you want to save monthly. 

7. Navigate to the next page by selecting Next Step.

Note: This page will show the the Set Categories window.

8. Under each category, toggle the slider to the amount you want to be allocated monthly for each of the budget categories.

9. Click Finalize.

 

Now you have successfully set up your account.
