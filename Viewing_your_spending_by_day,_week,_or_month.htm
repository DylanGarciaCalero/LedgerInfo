Viewing your spending by day, week, or month

You can view your spending habits in Ledger by date. This may be by day, week, or month. Viewing your spending in a visualized way can help you make better choices with your money.

To view your past spending entries by date:

 

1. In the homepage view, select All Expenses.

2. Click Time Period.

3. Select the time frame you want to view.

 

You will see your expenses laid out in a list formation according to date.
