Changing your email address

You can change your email address associated with your Ledger account. Follow these steps to update your contact information:

  1. Go to Ledger's website and log in.
  2. In the Homepage view, select the hamburger menu button. Click Profile.
  3. Add your email into the form.

Note: These steps will also allow you to change your name in your Ledger profile.

If you have followed these steps and were unable to successfully change your email, make sure your web browser’s cache is cleared.

Clearing your cache and cookies

This section will help you use Ledger more effectively online. Your internet browser stores data in a cache, which are usually records of recently visited websites as temporary files. When your browser’s cache is full or corrupted, it can cause problems with resetting your email or password.

Select your browser to learn how to clear your cache:

Chrome

  1. Open Chrome.
  2. Click More.
  3. Select More tools > Clear browsing data.
  4. Pick the date you want the content removed.
  5. Click the type of data you want removed.
  6. Select Clear data.

Internet Explorer

  1. In Internet Explorer, click Tools.
  2. Select Safety.
  3. Choose Delete browsing history.
  4. Click More.
  5. Select More tools > Clear browsing data.
  6. Pick the time frame, like All time, or Today.
  7. Click the type of data you want removed.
  8. Select Clear data.

Mozilla Firefox

  1. Within Firefox, select the Menu button.
  2. Click Settings.
  3. Select Privacy & Security.
  4. Find Cookies and Site Data section and click Clear Data.
  5. De-select Cookies and Site Data.
  6. Select Cached Web Content.
  7. Click Clear.

Safari

  1. Open the Safari app, select History > Clear History.
  2. Click the pop-up menu.
  3. Select each item and website you want removed.
