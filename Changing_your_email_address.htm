Changing your email address

You can change your email address associated with your Ledger account. Follow these steps to update your contact information:

 

1. Go to Ledger's website and log in.

2. Select your profile icon.

3. Enter your new email address into the New email form.

4. Select submit.

 

Note: These steps will also allow you to change your name in your Ledger profile.

 

If you have followed these steps and were unable to successfully change your email, make sure your web browser’s cache is cleared. Go to Clearing your cache and cookies for more information.
