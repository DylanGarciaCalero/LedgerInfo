Protecting your account

Protecting your online accounts is important for all forms of online profiles and applications you use. We suggest these best practices for protecting your Ledger account.

 

Practice

Details

Why

Create a complex password

Letters, numbers, and symbols

A complex password is a strong one. It must contain a randomized variety of letters, numbers, and symbols to make it harder for threats to guess the password and gain access to your account.

Change your password often

Every 6 to 8 months.

Data breaches occur sometimes to companies and their technology platforms.

If your account was part of a data breach on another platform, hackers will try to gain access to your account through trying the same password on another site, like Ledger. To avoid this, change your password often.

Keep your password stored away in a safe location

Online or offline.

If you write down your passwords on paper, make sure it is stored away safe where no one will find it.
