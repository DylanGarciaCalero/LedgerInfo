Best practices for achieving your saving goals

With Ledger you can track all of your financial decisions. Setting goals for saving will help you save money, and we've compiled a list of best practices for achieving your saving goals.

 

Practice

Details

Be specific

Knowing how much to save is important. When you are specific about what, when, and how, your saving goals become easier to achieve.

Create a timeline or a deadline

A timeline keeps you in-check and motivated. It also helps you know when you reach the goalposts and benchmarks.

Set monthly goals

Setting monthly goals is how you stay on top of accumulating savings.

Track as much as you can

All bank transactions are all tracked electronically. It is beneficial to have a place where you personally document all your transactions outside of your bank and shopping transactions, as this creates good habits for being aware of your money. When you personally document what you’re spending your money on, you become more aware of how to manage and hold onto it.

Create different naming for each goal

It is recommended that you set aside different accounts for each goal.
