How do I delete my personal information?

Your personal information is always secured and kept safe. We do not sell or share your data with any other organization or entity.

Keeping your information stored in Ledger is important because it helps us recover your account in case you can’t sign in.

If you wish to continue removing your personal information from Ledger, follow these steps:

 

  1. Go to Ledger's Homepage and log in.
  2. In the Homepage view, select the hamburger menu button. Click Profile.
  3. Remove your name or change email from the form.

 

You will now have a Ledger account without your personal details.
