Welcome to Ledger Jadetree 1.0 help site.

 

Ledger is a web-based application for managing your daily spending habits. You can access Ledger by visiting Ledger's homepage.

 

Ledger enables you to create savings goals for specific big-ticket items, like a vacation, a car, or paying off debt. You are shown your monetary goals a regular basis to keep you on track.

 

Choose a daily or weekly amount to store away into the savings goal. As time progresses, you are shown how much has been contributed into the savings goal, encouraging you to continue saving and eventually reaching your financial goals.
